# Code of Conduct

This project uses a simple standard: be useful, direct, and respectful.

## Expected behavior

- Focus criticism on code, docs, tests, and product outcomes.
- Assume contributors are here to improve the project.
- Keep discussions welcoming to new frontend, data, and prediction market builders.
- Disclose conflicts of interest when discussing builder fees, grants, or products.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Spam, pump-and-dump promotion, or misleading grant claims.
- Sharing private keys, credentials, or sensitive user data.
- Pressuring users into trades or financial decisions.

Maintainers may moderate issues, discussions, and pull requests to protect the
project and its contributors.
